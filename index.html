About Me

Migrant from the arts into tech, packing up my creative side to take with me. SAG/AFTRA professional performer with IMDb credits as Consulting Editor on a feature film.

I have produced and written web commercials, and have self-produced, narrated, edited and mastered audiobooks for Audible and Scholastic, have written everything from plays to product descriptions with SEO terms for Walmart. A lowbrow/highbrow bilingual/bicultural US citizen, translating medical case studies as well as subtitles for Turkish soap operas into English.

As a business roleplayer, I work with two London-based organizations that coach executives in leadership and communication--clients include Microsoft, Aetna, Barclay's, Ortho Diagnostics and New York Medical College. Looking forward to streamlining my various skills into one comprehensive web development position in a multi-media, globally engaged company.
